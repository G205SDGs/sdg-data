The task of the Federal Statistical Office is to provide and publish statistical information. This information must be objective, independent and of high quality. It is available to all interested parties: including policymakers, public administration, business, academia, and the general public. The legal basis and tasks of the Federal Statistical Office are laid down in the <a href="https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/bstatg_1987/BJNR004620987.html" target="_blank" onclick="return confirm_alert('the Federal Ministry of Justice','En');">Federal Statistics Act</a> (Bundesstatistikgesetz, BStatG).
