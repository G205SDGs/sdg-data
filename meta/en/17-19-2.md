In Germany, the <a href="https://www.gesetze-im-internet.de/pstg/" target="_blank" onclick="return confirm_alert('the Federal Ministry of Justice','En');">Civil Status Act (Personenstandsgesetz, PStG)</a> regulates both births and deaths. The birth of a child must be reported to the relevant registry office within one week. This regulation applies to all children born in Germany. A death must be reported no later than the third working day after the death.
