Disaster control in Germany is based on federal and state regulations. All 16 Länder have their own <a href="https://www.bundestag.de/resource/blob/916926/a4a75c813172c7ccdca7290c4c97dc82/WD-3-112-22-pdf-data.pdf" target="_blank" onclick="return confirm_alert ('the Federal Office of Civil Protection and Disaster Assistance','En');">Civil Protection Laws</a>, meaning that 100% of municipalities in Germany are subject to these legal requirements.
